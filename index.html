Intro

I'm now a Ph.D. student at Shenzhen International Graduate School(SIGS), Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Wenbo Ding. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in EE department from Tsinghua University in 2023.

My research interest includes Embodied AI, Multi-modal Sensing and Robot Manipulation, especially in Dexterous Manipulation. I believe that bionic robots of high DoCs are the true future of universal robots. My goal (in research) is to become a sciengineer, bridging the gap between theory and engineering.
